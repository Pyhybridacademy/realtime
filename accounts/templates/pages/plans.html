Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our trading plans.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing cycle. When downgrading, the new rate will apply at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 7-day free trial for all new users. You can explore our platform and features without any commitment. No credit card is required to start your trial.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and various cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin, Ethereum, and USDT.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period, and you won't be charged again.
