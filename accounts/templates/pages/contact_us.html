What are your customer support hours?

Our customer support team is available Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM in each of our office time zones. For urgent matters, we offer 24/7 support via our emergency hotline.

How quickly can I expect a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. For urgent matters, our response time is typically within 2-4 hours during business hours.

Do you offer technical support for the trading platform?

Yes, we provide comprehensive technical support for our trading platform. Our dedicated technical team can assist with installation, configuration, and troubleshooting issues.
